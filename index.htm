About Our School

Don Bosco Technical School, Kalimpong is committed to providing quality technical education with strong moral foundation.
We focus on hands-on training, industry-ready skills, and character development.
Our alumni are placed across top industries and organizations.


Don Bosco Technical institute, Kalimpong

Don Bosco Technical School, Kalimpong is a Salesian institution dedicated to the education and skill development of young people, especially the less privileged.

Established: Serving the youth of Kalimpong for several decades with a focus on technical training and character formation.

Vision: To form honest citizens and skilled professionals rooted in values, discipline, and service.

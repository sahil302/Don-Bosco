About Our Institute

Who We Are

Don Bosco Technical Institute, Kalimpong is a Salesian-run technical school dedicated to educating and skilling young people.
With decades of service, our institution stands as a pillar of discipline, training and character formation.
Our students and alumni are making their mark across industries, workshops and corporate sectors.


Vision • Mission • Values

Our Vision

To form technically skilled and value-oriented individuals committed to excellence and service.

Our Mission

- Deliver quality technical training
- Build discipline & character
- Promote holistic student development
- Prepare students for industry demands

Our Core Values

Integrity • Compassion • Discipline • Excellence • Respect
